Booyahs Must See

Horror Movies

This exercise was presented as a "Top 3 Best Ever"...blah blah blah.

I don't do that. I like it or I don't. There is no "Best" or "Worst."

These are people trying to make art. Do you know how hard that is?

It's really really hard. Admire anyone who tries.

Audition

The only horror film I've ever come close to walking out of. Not because it was bad. It was too good.

I made it, but only by watching the last 20 minutes through my fingers.

One Cut of the Dead

A low budget J Horror zombie film with amazing layers.

True to their word, the first 40 minutes of the film is one continuous take.

The Babadook

Not just a horror movie, but a nice exploration of grief, and its imposing presence in our lives.
